Family & Home

When I'm not orchestrating enterprise-scale infrastructure, my world revolves around my family. I love spending time with my wife and our three amazing kids. My son is a true inspiration—a craniopharyngioma survivor who reminds me every day of what resilience really looks like.

Our home is always full of life (and a bit of chaos), thanks to our vibrant crew of three dogs and two cats. They keep us on our toes and make sure there's never a dull moment.

Hobbies & Interests

I thrive on finding a balance between the digital and the natural world. Here's what I love doing to recharge:

  • The Great Outdoors: Whether it's a quiet hike or just taking in the fresh air, I love being outdoors.
  • Culinary Adventures: I have a real passion for cooking. There's something incredibly satisfying about bringing ingredients together to create a great meal.
  • Casual Gaming: I enjoy unwinding with a good game. I'm a casual gamer at heart and still have a major soft spot for classic side-scrollers.
  • Reading: A good book is the best escape. My absolute favorites are Into the Wild and The Untethered Soul.

Fun Facts

A few more things that make me, well, me:

  • Live Music: I am a huge fan of Nine Inch Nails—I've seen them live 23 times!
  • Ink: I have a number of tattoos, each with its own story and meaning.

My Philosophy

I believe in keeping things simple and positive, both in my personal life and in my career.

  • Daily Goal: I strive to accomplish at least one thing a day to be proud of.
  • The Golden Rule: Put good into the world, and good will return.
  • The Dream: I have a dream of a frictionless environment where all personalities and opinions can live in blissful harmony—a lofty goal, maybe, but one worth working toward every single day.
